Welcome to DINOLANDIA

DINOLANDIA is a resort located on a small island off the coast of Argentina. Our unique stays include real live dinosaurs for the ultimate prehistoric experience.

Accommodations

Our resorts boasts 127 rooms, 13 suites, and 2 habitats with sleeping pods, so you can slumber with the Sauropods!

Availability

We have very few spaces and reservations tend to be booked quickly, so please reserve a spot well in advance. If you would like to inquire about reserving a room, suite, or sleeping pod in our Sauropod exhibit, please reach out via our contact page.
